Instructions for Uploading Scholarships

To add more data to the scholarship database, create an excel sheet with the following columns in order:

  1. Scholarship Name
  2. Scholarship Description
  3. Scholarship Deadline (M-D-Y) (Excel should autoformat dates correctly)
  4. Award Amount (Format amount without dollar signs or commas)
  5. Category (Needs to match one of the following exactly:)
    • African-American
    • Agriculture
    • Arts-related
    • Asian
    • Asian Pacific American
    • Community Service
    • Construction Related Fields
    • Disabled
    • Engineering
    • Environmental Interest
    • Female
    • Filipino
    • First Generation College Student
    • Queer
    • General
    • Latinx
    • Immigrant/AB540/DACA
    • Interest in Journalism
    • Japanese
    • Jewish
    • Indigenous
    • Science/Engineering
    • Student-Athlete
    • Teaching
    • Women in Math/Engineering
  6. Merit Based (Yes or No)
  7. Service Based (Yes or No)
  8. Need Based (Yes or No)
  9. Minimum GPA (Excel should autoformat dates correctly)
  10. Interview Required (Yes or No)
  11. Link

Enter data as in the sample image above in an excel file. When you are finished entering all the data, save your excel sheet as a CSV file, then upload it to this portal and click "Submit."
