Next Generation Scholars Portal

The best way for students and counselors to communicate for the college application process


{% if not current_user.is_authenticated %} Get Started {% endif %}

Administrators

Administrators can invite and manage users, as well as add colleges and scholarships. Administrators can assign default tasks to students, view and manage student profiles, evaluate progress on important college application steps, such as recommendation letters and supplemental essays, as well as access and update the calendar. Counselors also have the option to add new colleges and scholarships for students to explore. {{ page.render_inline_editor(editable_html_obj, current_user) }}

Counselors

Counselors can assign default tasks to students, view and manage student profiles, evaluate progress on important college application steps, such as recommendation letters and supplemental essays, as well as access and update the calendar. Counselors also have the option to add new colleges and scholarships for students to explore, in addition to recommending colleges and scholarships to certain students. {{ page.render_inline_editor(editable_html_obj, current_user) }}

Students

Students can create a profile with contact information, grades and test scores, extracurriculars, colleges and majors of interest, recommendation letter and supplemental essay progress, as well as college acceptances and scholarship awards. Students can also keep track of their tasks through the checklist and calendar, as well as explore potential colleges and scholarships. {{ page.render_inline_editor(editable_html_obj, current_user) }}
